  • A ticket admits one named attendee. You are responsible for the accuracy of the details you provide.

3. Payment

Payment is processed securely by Stripe. Your booking is confirmed once payment is received and you receive a confirmation email. The Organiser does not store your card details.

4. Cancellations, refunds & transfers

  • Tickets are transferable to another person before the event — contact us to update the attendee's name.

8. Health & participation
