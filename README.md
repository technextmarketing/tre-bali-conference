# TRE Worldwide Conference — Bali 2027

Event website for the first worldwide TRE® conference, Bali, 12–15 November 2027.
Built by TechNext from the approved proposal deck design.

## Pages
- `index.html` — home / landing (hero, live countdown, tickets, speakers, programme, gallery)
- `tickets.html` — ticket waves, what's included, ticket FAQ
- `speakers.html` — speaker profiles, sessions, social links
- `event-info.html` — dates, venue, programme, travel, visa & invitation, hotels, FAQ
- `gallery.html` — image gallery (lightbox)
- `get-ticket.html` — registration form (tier select, visa question, Stripe-ready — demo)
- `privacy.html` — privacy policy (draft)
- `terms.html` — terms & conditions (draft)

## Notes
- Design system in `assets/style.css`; behaviour (countdown, nav, lightbox, form) in `assets/app.js`.
- Live countdown targets 2027-11-12 09:00 Bali time (WITA, UTC+8).
- Pricing shown is **indicative**; final tiers/prices confirmed at launch.
- The registration form is a front-end demo. Live version connects to Stripe checkout + Odoo CRM.
- Legal pages are drafts for TRE Indonesia to finalise.
