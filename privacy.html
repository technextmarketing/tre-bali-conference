Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data when you visit this site or register for the conference.

Draft for review · last updated November 2026. Final wording and the legal entity's details are confirmed by TRE Indonesia before launch.

1. Who we are

The TRE Worldwide Conference ("the Conference", "we", "us") is organised and hosted by TRE Indonesia. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website and event registration, how we use it, and your rights. Questions can be sent to hello@technext.asia.

2. Information we collect

  • Registration details — your name, email, phone/WhatsApp, country of residence, ticket selection and any notes you provide.
  • Visa & travel information — whether you require a visa for Indonesia, so we can issue an invitation letter where needed.
  • Payment information — card payments are processed by our payment provider, Stripe. We do not see or store your full card details.
  • Usage data — basic, aggregated information about how the site is used, to help us improve it.

3. How we use your information

  • To process your ticket, confirm your place and send your info pack.
  • To prepare visa invitation letters when requested.
  • To keep you informed about the event you registered for (schedule, logistics, important updates).
  • To meet our legal, accounting and safety obligations as an event organiser.

4. Sharing your information

We share data only where necessary to run the event: with our payment provider (Stripe) to take payment, and with trusted service providers who help us operate the Conference. We do not sell your personal data. Where an invitation letter is issued, relevant details are shared with the issuing entity, TRE Indonesia.

5. International transfers

As a global event, your information may be processed in countries outside your own. Where this happens, we take steps to ensure your data remains protected to an appropriate standard.

6. Data retention

We keep your registration data for as long as needed to deliver the event and to meet legal and accounting requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@technext.asia.

8. Cookies

This site uses only the minimal cookies needed to function and to understand aggregate usage. It does not use advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
