Voices of the gathering

The people guiding keynotes, workshops and practice across the 3.5 days. More speakers are announced as the programme is confirmed.

Host & Founder

Isabelle Claus Teixeira

Isabelle is the founder of the global TRE® network and a leadership & somatic coach based between Singapore and Europe. She has spent years building communities of practitioners across continents.

At the conference she hosts the opening and sets the tone for a gathering built on connection, nervous-system awareness and shared practice.

Opening keynote: Bringing the worldwide TRE® community together
Facilitator

Samara B. Ryon

Samara is a holistic coach, certified TRE® provider and ReUnity MATRIX® facilitator. Her work blends somatic practice with a gentle, embodied approach to release and integration.

She leads hands-on workshops and practice sessions designed to help participants feel the work in their own bodies.

Workshop: From shaking to shaping — somatic release in practice
