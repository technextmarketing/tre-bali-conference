Ticket FAQ

Good to know

How is payment handled?

Tickets are paid securely by card through Stripe, in US dollars. Your card details are never stored on this site.

Can I get a refund?

Refund and transfer terms are set out in our Terms & Conditions. In short, tickets are transferable to another person and refundable up to a cut-off date before the event.

Do prices really rise?

Yes. Tickets sell in waves — when a wave sells out, the next opens at a higher price. Booking early is the best way to pay less.

Will there be an online option?

An online / video-access ticket may be offered as a later phase. Register your interest via Get your ticket and we'll keep you posted.

I need a visa for Indonesia — can you help?

Yes. When you book we ask if you need a visa, and TRE Indonesia can issue an official invitation letter to support your application. See Visa & invitation.
