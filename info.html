Getting there

Travel & arrival

  • Fly into Denpasar (DPS) — Ngurah Rai International Airport, Bali's main gateway.
  • Arrive by Day 1 afternoon — check-in from 15:00, ahead of the welcome circle.
  • Airport transfers — guidance and recommended options are included in your info pack.

Visa & invitation letters

Many visitors can enter Indonesia on a visa on arrival or e-visa. When you book, we ask whether you need a visa — and if so, TRE Indonesia can issue an official invitation letter to support your application, with no chasing required.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Who is the conference for?

TRE® practitioners, facilitators, trainees and anyone drawn to somatic, nervous-system-based practice. All levels are welcome.

What's included in my ticket?

Full access to all 3.5 days — keynotes, workshops, practice sessions and community events — plus a digital info pack and certificate. See Tickets.

Are meals and accommodation included?

Programme refreshments are provided; travel, hotels and meals outside the programme are arranged by you. We send a recommended hotel list on booking.

Do I need a visa?

It depends on your nationality. When you register we ask if you need one, and TRE Indonesia can issue an invitation letter to support your application.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing for movement, a water bottle, and an open mind. A full "what to pack" note is in your info pack.

Is there an online option?

An online / video-access ticket may be offered as a later phase — register your interest via Get your ticket.
